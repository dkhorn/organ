# Ranks - by windchest

## Casavant Great Slider
These are not individually addressable and cannot be borrowed other than via coupling
- 8' Flute a Cheminee - 61 notes - (Chimney Flute - 4' physical length)
- 4' Prestant - 61 notes
- 4' Petite Flute - 61 notes
- Cornet II - 61 notes (122 pipes)
- Mixture IV - 61 notes (244 pipes)

## Casavant Great Facade
These are all individually addressable and can be borrowed, unified, etc
- 16' Bourdon (metal, stopped, 8' physical length) - 61 notes
- 8' Montre (metal, open, 8' physical length) - 61 notes

## Casavant Positiv Slider
These are not individually adressable and cannot be borrowed other than via coupling
- 8' Bourdon (metal, stopped, 4' physical length) - 61 notes
- 4' Flute a Fuseau - 61 notes
- 2 2/3' Nasard - 61 notes
- 2' Doublette - 61 notes
- 8' Cromorne (reed - half-length, 4' physical length) - 61 notes

## Casavant Pedal facade
Individually addressable, can be borrowed, unified, etc
- 16' Soubasse (wood, 8' physical length) - 32 notes
- 8' Octave Basse (metal principal-type) - 44 notes - (also used as a "4' Octave" stop)
- 16' Bombarde (reed, metal, 8' physical length) - 32 notes

## Casavant Swell Slider
Not individually addressable
- 8' flute ourverte (metal, open, 8' length) - 61 notes
- 4' Prestant (metal, open,  4' length) - 61 notes
- Mixture II - 61 notes (122 pipes)

## Casavant Swell Unit
All individually addressable, borrowable, etc
- 8' trumpet (reed, metal, 8' length) - 61 notes - also used as 4' clarion stop
- 16' Bassoon/ 8' Oboe (reed, metal, half-length resonators (longest is 8')) - 73 notes
- 8' Viole de Gambe (flute, metal, open, 8' length) - 61 notes - also used as 4' Viole stop
- 8' Fl. Douce (flute, metal, conical, 8' length) - 73 notes - also used as 16' and 4' stops
- 8' Celeste (flute, metal, open, Tenor C (longest pipe is 4')) - 49 notes

## Casavant Swell extensions
All individually addressable, borrowable, etc
- 4' Flute conique - 61 notes
- 8' Vox Humana - 61 notes

## Wicks Unit chest
All individually addressable, borrowable, etc
- 8' Stopped Flute (wood, stopped, physical length 4') - 85 notes - can be unified 8', 4', or 2', or mutations
- 4' Salicional - (metal, open) 61 notes
- 4' Celeste - (metal, open) 49 notes (no top octave)

## Wicks/Other principal chorus chest
All individually addressable - but the mixture pipes are combined per note into a single valve
- 4' Open Diapason (Metal, open, Wicks)- 61 notes
- 2' fifteenth (Metal, open, not Wicks) - 61 notes
- Mixture IV (Metal, open, not Wicks) - 61 notes (244 pipes)

## Wicks Gedeckt
individually addressable
- 16' Gedeckt (Stopped flute, physical length 8', bass octave on extension chest) - 97 notes - can be unified 16', 8', 4', 2, or mutations

## Ancient Bourdon
- 16' large-scale bourdon (wood, stopped, 8' physical length) - 32 notes (pedal)


# Divisions
Here's how the above windchests will be physically arranged by division

Great/Pedal (unenclosed):
- Casavant Great Slider
- Casavant Great Facade
- Casavant Pedal facade
- Ancient Bourdon

Positiv (unenclosed):
- Casavant Positiv Slider
- Wicks/Other principal chorus chest

Swell/Recit (enclosed, with operable shades):
- Casavant Swell Slider
- Casavant Swell Unit
- Casavant Swell Extensions
- Wicks Gedeckt

Echo/Choir:
- Wicks Unit chest
